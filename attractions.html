ATTRACTIONS

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano.
However, there are many other things to do!

The island offers something for everyone, including a local history museum, snorkeling, several pubs, a microbrewery, dance club, movie theater, arcade, art galleries, and a bowling alley. Also, a nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year. Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. Taniti City boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Other popular activities include boat or bus tours of the island, guided hikes or zip-lining in the rainforest, chartered fishing tours, helicopter rides, and visits to Taniti’s active volcano.
